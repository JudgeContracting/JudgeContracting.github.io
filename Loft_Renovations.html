Loft Renovations & Lease Space Renovations


Loft Renovations (Old Sweat Shop Remodeling)

A loft can be used in its entirety as a living space or subdivided into multiple living spaces.

Whether or not you subdivide the loft space you will need to consider each space as it’s own living area. Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC will all need to be taken into consideration per space.

Each space would require it’s own custom living design (kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living room, dining room, home office, home theatre, den, solarium, etc.). Or you can go with the now fashionable open concept.


Renovations for Lease Space in Your Private Home

Are you a micro entrepreneur, such as an AirBnb host, looking into renovating a privately owned space for leasing?

The best way to consider everything involved in renovating a lease space in your home is to think of everything a guest will need as a home away from home.

If your looking to lease space in your private home you want to consider a few things:

  • The possibility of a private entry

  • Whether or not a guest will have a private bath or if they just needs access to the family bath

If the lease space is large enough you may want to convert it into an efficieny apartment. Which would include a small private bath, private kitchen, and a bedroom/living combination. You may accomplish this with a garage conversion.


Remember, that any space can be converted into a living area.

If you’re thinking of the possibility of any of these concepts we can help you with the choice of design and materials. For more information please feel free to call us anytime!
