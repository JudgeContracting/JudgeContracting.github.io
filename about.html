A relocation to Houston in the early 1970â€™s, prompted a company name change to the current Judge Contracting, as we used our expertise in wood flooring to ease into the construction industry in a new city. Having left two of his brothers to pursue different career directions, Mark Payne, who started with the company at the tender age of nine years old, took over the business from his father in 1973, preserving a continuously operated family-business for the past 55 years.
