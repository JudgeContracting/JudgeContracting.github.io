Floor Stripping, Waxing, and Maintenance

When, Why, How

Wood floors and tiled floors will from time to time need to be stripped of their old wax and re-coated with fresh wax to maintain their health as well as looks.

Sometimes the question arises: When should I have my floor stripped and waxed? One of the best ways is by consultation from a professional. Judge Contracting offers free consultations on floor maintenance.

Email us a photo (more than one if you would like) and some information on the age of the floor, and the common causes of wear on the floor. You can email us anytime at: judgecontracting@yahoo.com

Or if you have a larger space that needs physical consultation, we would be more than happy to come out and assess it and let you know what your floor might need.

Feel free to contact us anytime!
